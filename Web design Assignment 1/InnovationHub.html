What we offer

The Innovation Hub's primary goal is to connect aspiring innovators and business owners in Massachusetts with the funding, business mentoring, and R&D tools they need to launch a new enterprise.

Available resources at the Innovation Hub are as follows:

  1. Separate workspaces and assigned desks
  2. Coworking spaces that are shared
  3. Meeting spaces equipped with audiovisual equipment
  4. WiFi internet connectivity at a high speed
  5. Safe parking and access available around-the-clock
  6. Regional buses and MBTA commuter rail are accessible.
